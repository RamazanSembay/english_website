My name is Nazym!

Hi! Nice to meet you! My name is Nazym Akhmetzhan. I am 19 and a student in university. I go to university in Astana. My favorite courses are Art, English, and Chinese. Chinese is my hardest course. My professors are very friendly and smart. It’s my third year in university now. I love it!

I live in a big flat on Kabanbai Batyr Street. It’s near the university campus. I share the house with two other students. Their names are Diana and Kamila. We help each other with homework. On the weekend, we cook and clean together.

I have a brother. He just started high school. He is 14 and lives with my parents. They live on Dostyk street in Almaty. Sometimes they visit me in Astana. I am happy when they visit. My Mom always brings me sweets and candy when they come. I really miss them, too!

The pet store

First, she sees dogs and cats. Baby dogs are called puppies. Baby cats are called kittens. Anel likes them because they are easy to take care of and can play a lot, but they will get bigger. Anel wants a small pet. Then Anel sees animals that have to live in a cage. She sees rabbits, guinea pigs, and mice. Mice are what you call more than one mouse. She likes these animals because they are small. Birds live in cages too. Anel sees a parrot and a canary. She likes them all, but she doesn’t want to clean the cage. Anel sees the animals in tanks. The tanks full of water have fish and turtles in them. She thinks about a goldfish but decides she likes the turtles more. There are also tanks with rocks and sand that have snakes, spiders, and scorpions, but Anel is afraid of them. Anel likes the turtles best, but they won’t fit in the little fishbowls. Turtles need bigger tanks, so they can swim sometimes and hide sometimes. Anel decides she wants to get a turtle for her birthday.
